Indiana Jones

Raiders of the Lost Ark

Based on the hit ride at Disneyland, Raiders of the Lost Ark is the story of how infamous smuggler and scoundrel Han Solo goes on one last mission for his dastardly employer Jabba the Hutt. He must retrieve and smuggle the Lost Ark back to Tatooine, but he must also change his entire outfit to avoid suspicion on this strange planet.

Temple of Doom

After the runaway success of the sequel to the hit Disneyland ride, George Lucas directed another film in the Indiana Jones saga. This time, Han Solo is tasked with completing a series of action sequences that do not advance the plot at all, but that Imagineers all agreed would make for a thrilling ride.

The Last Crusade

After the Disneyland ride for Temple of Doom was closed down due to a terrible tragedy, George Lucas believed that a new film vaguely related to religious persecution while starring Han Solo would be the film that opened up the possibility of getting the greenlight for his dream project:
Star Wars: THE RIDE
(The tragedy was not related to the Temple of Doom ride, but it affected the nation so deeply that nobody could in good conscience continue enjoying what they once did.)
